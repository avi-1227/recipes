Yogurt Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender
  2. blend until smooth
